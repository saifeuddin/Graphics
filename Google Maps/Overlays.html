Google Maps -Overlays

Overlays are object on the map that are bound to latitude/ longitude coordinates.

Google Maps have several overlays:
  1. Marker:

    Single locations on a map.

  2. Polyline:

    Series of strait lines on a map.

  3. Polygon

    Series of strait lines on a map and the shape is "closed".

  4. Info Window:

    Displays content within a popup ballon on the top of a map.

  5. Circle and Rectangle
  6. Custom overlays
